Patch notes format

How to summarize updates

Each update entry should explain what changed, who it affects, and whether older guides need revisions. Keep it short and link outward when a change needs a full article.

Known issues

Why bug pages matter

Players often search when something feels broken. A lightweight issues section can answer that intent faster than a general guide.

  • Describe the issue in plain language.
  • State whether it seems temporary or confirmed.
  • List any workaround if one exists.
