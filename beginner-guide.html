Heartopia beginner guide for the first hour and first day

This page is designed to become the main entry article for new-player search traffic. It should give a simple route, not an overwhelming wiki dump.

What this page should solve

  1. What to do in the first hour
  2. What to unlock first
  3. How to spend early resources
  4. Which mistakes slow progress down

Quick answer

The best beginner guide starts with immediate priorities, not lore. A strong version of this article should tell players the first systems to unlock, the first resources to keep, and the first traps to avoid wasting time on.

Suggested structure

  1. Open with a one-paragraph first-hour plan.
  2. Explain the first upgrade path and why it matters.
  3. List early mistakes like overspending or ignoring key systems.
  4. Link out to the daily checklist and resource pages.

Notes for later writing

Add screenshots of the first important menus and locations.

Include a “last updated” date once the guide has real gameplay details.

Break long sections into short subheads for search readability.
