About the site

What Heartopia Strategy is and who it is for

Heartopia Strategy is an independent English-language guide site built to help Heartopia players find beginner help, daily routine tips, resource lookup pages, FAQs, and update notes in a simpler format than long forum threads or scattered social posts.

Who runs this website

This website is maintained by an independent publisher and is intended as a fan-made game guide resource. It is not an official website of the Heartopia developers or publishers.

What kind of content is published here

The site focuses on practical strategy content, including beginner routes, daily checklists, materials and locations, troubleshooting notes, and short answers to common player questions.

Editorial approach

The goal of the site is to publish useful, easy-to-scan content that helps players make progress faster. Articles may be updated over time as game systems change, new features arrive, or better information becomes available.
