Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains the general types of information that may be collected when you use Heartopia Strategy and how that information may be used.

This website may collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, approximate location by region, referring pages, and general usage data through hosting services, analytics tools, or standard server logs.

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand site performance, improve user experience, and measure traffic. If analytics or advertising services are added in the future, those services may also use cookies or related tracking methods.

  • Cookies may be used to remember settings or measure engagement.

  • These tools may collect aggregated information rather than direct personal identity.

If you contact the website by email, the information you provide such as your email address, message content, and related communication details may be used to respond to your request, review corrections, or handle site-related questions.

Advertising

This website may display third-party advertising in the future, including Google AdSense or similar advertising partners. These providers may use cookies or web beacons to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads.

  • Users may learn more about Google advertising practices through Google's own policies.

How information may be used

Information collected through this website may be used to operate the site, improve guide content, understand traffic patterns, maintain security, respond to inquiries, and support advertising or analytics services if those features are enabled.

External links

Pages on this website may link to third-party websites. Heartopia Strategy is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

Your choices

You can choose to limit cookies through your browser settings. You may also avoid sending personal information by not contacting the site directly through email. If third-party analytics or advertising services are in use, their own settings and policies may offer additional controls.

Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the website changes, including if analytics, advertising tools, new content features, or contact methods are added.
